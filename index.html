Yi Luo

I am a 4th year graduate student from National University of Singapore under supervision of Wee Teck Gan. Before that, I obtained my B.Sc. from Peking University.

I am working on representation theory and number theory, especially in Langlands program, automorphic representations and Galois cohomology.

Currently, I am exploring several aspects of disconnected reductive groups over p-adic fields and number fields. Tasho Kaletha has formulated an LLC conjecture for disconnected reductive groups (of certain type).

(1) Following Kaletha's pioneering work, we are attempting to formulate a general conjectural global multiplicity formula (of determining multiplicity of automorphic representations in the discrete spectrum in terms of the LLC) for disconnected reductive groups.

(2) As evidence, for disconnected tori, we have established a multiplicity formula, using Galois (hyper)cohomology and a reformulation of Kaletha's LLC for disconnected tori. Another evidence comes from the known results of Arthur's multiplicity formula for orthogonal groups.

(3) I am currently learning moduli interpretation of LLC from [FS21] and [DHKM20], etc., and another goal I am pursuing is to extend their work to disconnected setting.
